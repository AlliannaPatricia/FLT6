Finals Lab Task 6

Working with MongoDB

In this lab activity, I explored basic MongoDB operations such as inserting documents, querying data, performing updates, deletions, text searches, and managing relationships between collections. The goal was to simulate real-world scenarios like managing movies, users, posts, and comments.

Steps Followed

  • Step 1 – Insert Documents: Used insertMany() to populate movie and user collections.
  • Step 2 – Queries: Queried documents using find() with filters (writer, actor, franchise, year).
  • Step 3 – Updates: Used update() with $set to add fields (e.g., synopses, actors).
  • Step 4 – Text Search: Used regular expressions to include/exclude keywords like “Bilbo” and “Gandalf.”
  • Step 5 – Deletion: Used remove() to delete documents (e.g., specific movies).
  • Step 6 – Relationships: Inserted users, posts, and comments collections simulating relationships.
  • Step 7 – Querying Related Collections: Queried relational data between users, posts, and comments.
